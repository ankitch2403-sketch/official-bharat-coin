● What is BHRT

Built for Bharat

Bharat Coin (BHRT) is a decentralized BEP-20 token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain — one of the fastest and most affordable blockchain networks in the world.

With a fixed supply of 1 Billion tokens, BHRT is built on principles of transparency, decentralization, and community ownership. Every transaction is verifiable on-chain, with no hidden mechanisms.

Our vision is to build a strong community-driven ecosystem that celebrates the pride, culture, and digital future of Bharat.
